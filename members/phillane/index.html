About Me

Computer Science

  • High performance computing research assistant.

  • Theoretical computer scientist
    • Specializations in computability theory and programming language theory.
  • Programmer in over fifteen languages.
    • C#, Node.js, C, BASIC, C++, ARM Assembly, x86 Assembly, Python, F#, Java, CIL/MSIL Assembly, Visual Basic, Prolog, Go, Rust, Haskell, Lisp, and Scala
  • Special skills in many popular paradigms of programming.
    • Familiar with functional programming, object-oriented programming, and parallel programming, among others.
    • Especially familiar with parallel programming, with knowledge of OpenMP, OpenMPI, and OpenACC
  • Have written multiple programming languages.
    • An object-oriented language called N# which transpiles to SmileBASIC, a purely-functional language called H# which runs on an interpreter, and even an extention of the pbrain programming language called Unlimibrain.
